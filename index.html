About Lingalo

Lingalo is a professional-grade language learning tool designed for rapid mastery of Japanese and Korean script, as well as core vocabulary in French and Spanish.

The Branding

Our logo is an elephant because an elephant never forgets. This represents our commitment to helping you build long-term memory through scientifically-backed learning methods.

The Methodology

We combine Spaced Repetition (SRS) principles with immediate feedback and gamified XP progression. Whether you're learning Hiragana, Hangul, or European vocabulary, our system adapts to your personal performance to keep you in the "Goldilocks zone" of learning.

Privacy & Data

Your data belongs to you. Lingalo operates entirely in your browser; we do not use trackers, and we never store your progress on a server. Everything—your XP, levels, and custom vocabulary—is saved locally on your device via secure browser storage.

Legal & Compliance

Lingalo is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. All language data is curated for educational purposes. By using this site, you agree to local storage of your progress data.
